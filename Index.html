Relaxation and Sleep Aid

1. Breathing Exercise

Follow the prompts to inhale, hold your breath, and exhale. Sounds or vibrations will guide you.

2. Muscle Relaxation

Relax each part of your body sequentially, starting from your toes to your head.

3. White Noise

Play soothing natural sounds to calm your mind.

4. Meditation

Repeat the phrase "I am completely relaxed" in your mind while visualizing a serene lake.

5. Sleep Story

Imagine a peaceful story or request a custom bedtime story.
